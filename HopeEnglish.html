User Experience and Usability Evaluation for Hope English

Conducted a comprehensive evaluation on the mobile app of Hope English

Project Background : Course Project

Project date : 2018.2 - 2018.6

Project duration : 4 months

Project course : User Experience and Usability Evaluation

Project supervisor : Hope English's CFO, Jasper Chang & Dr. "Stanley" Yung-Ju Chang

A group project

What's the issue

We conducted a comprehensive evaluation on the mobile app of Hope English, the leading brand of English learning in Taiwan. Evaluating method conducted includes Interaction map, Interviews, Persona, Scenario, Comparative Analysis, Survey, Heuristic Evaluation, and Usability Evaluation.

How we address the issue

To understand the app thoroughly and provide insightful suggestion to improve the user experience, we utilized techniques mentioned below.

Interaction Map

In the Interaction Map, we analyzed the views of the app and the relationship between each views or activities. This step gave us a comprehensive understanding of the product.

Interview, Persona & Scenario

In the next phase, we have to determine the target audiance of the product. In order to do so, we conducted an Interview to understand peoples' incentive and behavior in online language learning activities. By digging in to their scenario, attitude and experience, we established three main Persona and Scenario of our target audiance.

Survey

A Survey was conducted following qualitative analysis to further collect the quantitative data. We collected and analyzed data of participants' Characteristics, Attitudes and Behaviors in online language learning activities. Ferthermore, we collect participants' self-report usability evaluation via System Usability Scale (SUS).

Comparative Analysis

At the same time, a Comparative Analysis is conducted to better understand how other comparators design their product. We compares our client's design with others' to learn form the good ones and avoid poor design choices.

Heuristic Evaluation

A Heuristic Evaluation is conducted by the researchers. Based on the 10 Heuristic principles established by Jakob Nielsen, me and my team set up scenarios, then each of us provided individual evaluation to the violations of heuristic principles met in the scenario. We then generated our findings after debriefing and discussion.

Usability Test

The last evaluation we conducted was Usability Test. Unlike the Heuristic Evaluation, Usability invite user to participate in our study experiment. The participant are asked to perform the tasks accoding to our plan. The researchers evaluated difficulties participants faces when performing tasks. Participants also been asked to respone short questionnaires and a comprehensive post-task questionnaire to provide their preceived experience during the task. We then generated our findings after data analysis and discussion.

What's my role in the project

I was a highly engaged member in our team. Me and my team member conducted all the evaluations mentioned above. I particularly dedicated myself in the design of interview and questionnaire questions. This experience improves my ability to capture people's true thoughts via asking the right questions.
